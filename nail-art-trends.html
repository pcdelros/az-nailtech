Nail art trends

Trendy nail ideas that are still wearable.

Use this page as an SEO-friendly inspiration guide for clients looking for their next set.

01

Blush chrome nails

A soft pink or nude base topped with pearl or chrome powder. It photographs well and works for everyday wear.

02

Micro French tips

A thinner, cleaner take on classic French. Best for clients who want a polished look without heavy art.

03

Aura nails

A diffused color glow layered over a sheer base. Great for clients who want something creative but soft.

04

Milky neutral sets

Sheer white, nude, or pink tones that make the nails look clean, bright, and minimal.

05

Fine-line details

Small stars, bows, dots, swirls, or metallic lines that add personality without overcrowding the nail.

Pick your vibe, then book the right design level.

If the design has chrome, French, aura effects, or multiple details, choose a higher design level in the booking request.
