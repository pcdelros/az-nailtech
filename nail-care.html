Nail care guide

How to prep for your appointment and protect your set after.

This SEO-friendly guide gives clients simple instructions without overwhelming them.

Before your appointment

  • Avoid picking or peeling old product.
  • Bring design inspiration if you want art.
  • Know your preferred shape and length.
  • Tell your nail tech about lifting, breaks, or sensitivity.

After your appointment

  • Use cuticle oil regularly.
  • Wear gloves for heavy cleaning.
  • Avoid using nails as tools.
  • Book maintenance before major lifting starts.

Why aftercare matters

Good nail retention depends on product application and daily habits. Even a well-done set can lift early if nails are used to scrape, pry, pick, or open packaging.

Do not pull product off at home. A careful removal appointment helps keep the next set easier to prep and apply.

When to schedule a fill

Most clients schedule maintenance around two to three weeks, depending on growth, product type, and lifestyle. Waiting too long can increase lifting and repair time.
