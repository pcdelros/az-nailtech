Nail services built around clean prep, wearable shape, and detailed art.

Natural nails

Gel Manicure

Cuticle care, shaping, gel polish, and a glossy finish for clients who want a clean everyday manicure.

  • Best for short to medium natural nails
  • Great for simple color, shimmer, or minimal art
  • Typical wear: 2 to 3 weeks depending on lifestyle

Strength

Builder Gel Overlay

A structured overlay for natural nails that need more strength while keeping the look slim and polished.

  • Best for growing natural nails
  • Supports almond, square, oval, and soft square shapes
  • Pairs well with chrome, French, and detailed art

Length

Soft Gel Extension Set

Extension-style service for clients who want instant length, shape, and a full design moment.

  • Best for medium to long sets
  • Works with trending designs and statement art
  • Requires proper removal to protect natural nails

Refresh

Fill, Repair, and Removal

Maintenance appointments for grown-out sets, small breaks, safe removal, and fresh design changes.

  • Recommended before lifting becomes severe
  • Repair pricing depends on nail condition
  • Removal includes careful product breakdown
